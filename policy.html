HairNote Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 22, 2026
Last updated: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how HairNote ("the App") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the App. For the purposes of this Policy, "we", "us", and "our" mean the publisher of HairNote as identified in the Apple App Store for your purchase or download territory.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to the HairNote mobile application on iOS. It does not apply to third-party websites, services, or applications that we do not control.

2. Information We Process

We process information to provide the App's features, including recording hair-related events, inspiration references, reminders, salon-style notes, optional voice notes, and in-app coin features.

2.1 Information stored primarily on your device

The App is designed to store most of your content locally on your device, including:

  • Text you enter (for example event details, preferences, restrictions, notes, and reminder settings).
  • Photos and images you add (for example before/after photos, reference images, and inspiration items saved in the App).
  • Voice recordings you create in the App.
  • Text produced from optional speech-to-text transcription of your voice notes, when you use that feature.
  • In-app coin balance and transaction records related to purchases, grants, refunds where applicable, and feature usage.

We use this information to operate the App (display, edit, save, and related functionality as implemented in the current version).

2.2 Sign in with Apple

If you choose Sign in with Apple, Apple may provide us with identifiers and optional profile information you agree to share (for example an Apple user identifier, and name or email if you choose to share them). We use this information to associate your local profile with your Apple account and to enable features that require sign-in.

Apple's processing of your information when you use Sign in with Apple is described in Apple's privacy policy.

2.3 In-app purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple. We may process purchase-related information on your device (for example product identifiers, transaction identifiers, purchase timestamps, and coin amounts granted) to deliver purchased content, prevent duplicate fulfillment, and provide support.

We do not receive your full payment card details from Apple.

2.4 Speech recognition

If you use optional speech-to-text, the App uses Apple's Speech framework to analyze audio files stored on your device. Depending on your device, settings, and Apple's implementation, audio or derived data may be processed on Apple's systems to perform recognition. Apple's privacy policy applies to that processing.

2.5 Notifications

If you enable notifications, the App may schedule local notifications based on your reminder settings. Notification permission is handled by iOS.

3. Permissions

The App may request access to the photo library, camera, microphone, speech recognition, and local notifications, as needed for the features described in the App and in the system permission prompts. You can grant or revoke these permissions in iOS Settings.

4. Tracking and analytics

The current version of the App does not use the App Tracking Transparency framework for cross-app advertising tracking, and does not integrate third-party advertising or analytics SDKs for that purpose as part of the App's distribution build described here.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may process information through Apple's services where you use features that rely on Apple (for example Sign in with Apple, App Store purchases, and speech recognition as described above).

6. Security and retention

We use reasonable measures consistent with a mobile application of this kind to protect information processed by the App. Because much of your content is stored on your device, you should protect your device with a passcode or biometric lock and keep your operating system updated.

Your information remains on your device unless you delete it within the App where supported, delete the App, or erase your device. If you use iCloud or other device backup features, backup copies may be created according to your device settings and Apple's terms.

7. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child in a way that requires parental action, contact us using the support options described on HairNote's App Store product page.

8. Your choices and rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, or restrict certain processing of personal information. You may exercise choices available to you through the App and iOS Settings (including permissions and account features). You may also contact us using the support options described on HairNote's App Store product page.

9. International users

If you use the App outside your home country, your information may be processed in the country where you use the device and in accordance with Apple's infrastructure for the services you choose to use.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the updated Policy with a new effective date in this document or otherwise provide notice as required by law. Continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated Policy where permitted by law.

11. Contact

You may contact us through the Contact or Support link shown on HairNote's App Store product page, or through any in-app support entry point we provide for HairNote.
